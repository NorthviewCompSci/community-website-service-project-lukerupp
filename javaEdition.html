Welcome to the Java Edition! The Java edition is available for PC only. To see if your PC or laptop supports it go to help.minecraft.net .

How to Join

To join, click on the servers option on the main menu and type in the IP minecraftnv.org . It will be automatically updated to the most recent official edition so ensure your version is up to date.
